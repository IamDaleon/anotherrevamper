Kava

(Piper Methysticum)

Is a plant native to the South Pacific islands. The active compounds in kava are called kavalactones, which are known to have muscle relaxant, anxiolytic (anti-anxiety), and analgesic (pain-relieving) properties.

Kava
(Piper Methysticum)

Is a plant native to the South Pacific islands. The active compounds in kava are called kavalactones, which are responsible for its physiological and psychological effects. These compounds are known to have muscle relaxant, anxiolytic (anti-anxiety), and analgesic (pain-relieving) properties.
